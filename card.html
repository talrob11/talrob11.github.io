Great White Shark

Great Whites actually have a social hierachy. Typically they're loners but female Great Whites often dominate males!

  • Scientific Name: Carcharodon carcharias
  • Length Range: 13 - 16 ft
  • Lifespan Range: 40 - 70 years old
  • Habitat: Coastal and Offshore waters

Great White Sharks typically have tough skin that's a dull gray color. Along with that they have muscular asymmetrical tails that are upturned, pointed fins, a pointed snout that extends forward, and sharp triangular teeth.
